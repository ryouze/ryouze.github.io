Tutorial

1. Open the Play tab (or click here).

2. Choose vocabulary using the menu at the bottom.

3. Enter translation for the word listed, and press Enter.

▷ If you don't know the word, simply press Enter without inputting anything.

▷ If you are lazy, you can type only the first letter of the answer (e.g., apple).

▷ If "Questions in both languages" is enabled, the questions will be served in both languages (50% chance).

▷ For answers, the Polish characters are NOT required - e.g., "anschließen" -> "polaczyc" (instead of "połączyć").
